Date: 22 Jun 2025
Duration: 45 minutes
Impact: Delayed bank payouts
Root Cause Analysis

An upstream maintenance window at IPSL caused intermittent timeouts. Our circuit breaker tripped correctly, but the fallback queue did not drain fast enough upon restoration due to a misconfigured rate limit on the worker pool.
