SLA Exclusions

The 99.9% Uptime SLA commitment excludes downtime resulting from the following scenarios:

  • Scheduled Maintenance: Maintenance windows communicated at least 7 days in advance.
  • Third-Party Outages: Outages caused by underlying telecom networks (e.g. Safaricom M-Pesa core downtime) or banking partners (PesaLink switch downtime).
  • Force Majeure: Events outside our reasonable control (natural disasters, nationwide internet outages, regulatory freezes).
  • API Abuse: Downtime resulting from your breach of our rate limits or acceptable use policy (e.g., volumetric DDoS attacks originating from your credentials).
  • Sandbox Environment: The Sandbox is provided "as-is" and is excluded from production SLA guarantees.

Uptime Calculation Methodology

Monthly Uptime Percentage is calculated using the following formula:

Uptime % = (Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month * 100
Definition of Downtime:

A minute is considered "Downtime" if the error rate (5XX HTTP status codes) for Core APIs exceeds 5% during that minute, measured server-side. Downtime excludes SLA Exclusions (e.g., planned maintenance, force majeure).

SLA Credits:

If uptime falls below 99.9% in a billing month, you are eligible for service credits applied to your next invoice. Credits must be claimed via a support ticket within 30 days of the affected month.
