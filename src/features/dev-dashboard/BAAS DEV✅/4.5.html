Run: Failed Payment + Retry

Tests your systems retry logic. Emits a payment.failed (Insufficient Funds) event, waits 5s, then emits a successful capture.

Creates a successful KES 2,000 charge, then issues a KES 500 partial refund via the API, emitting refund.processed.
